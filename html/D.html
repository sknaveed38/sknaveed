Nutrition

Green leafy vegetables

Green leafy vegetables have a great amount of calcium, which is vital to promote your bone health.

Such as: collard greens, spinach, turnip greens, kale, mustard greens, beet greens, bok choy and okra.

Recommended daily intake
Protein,Energy,Carbohydrate,Fibre

2-3 cups

Chicken breast

3.5 oz (100g) Chicken breast contains 24g of protein but nearly fat-free, helping in building your bones and muscle tissues.

Except boosting your height, Chicken breast can also protect your heart and prevent cardiovascular disease.

Recommended daily intake

5-6 oz (about 150-180g)

Eggs

Eggs contain 28g of protein, 2g of calcium, and 0.3g of iron. They are also a good source of fiber and vitamin D.

Eggs are a great way to increase your protein intake and keep your blood sugar levels in check.

Recommended daily intake

2 eggs

Nuts and seeds

Nuts and seeds are rich in vitamins A, C, and E, which are essential for maintaining good immune function. They also contain healthy fats and fiber.

Examples of nut and seed sources include almonds, peanuts, cashews, walnuts, pistachios, chia seeds, and flaxseeds.

Recommended daily intake

1 cup (250g) of nuts and seeds

bananas

Bananas are rich in vitamin C, fiber, and antioxidants. They also contain 1.5g of potassium and 0.4g of calcium.

Except eating them raw, a banana smoothie is also delicious!

Recommended daily intake

2-3 banana

Milk

Milk is rich in calcium. Calcium is essential to build strong bones. It's also rich in vitamin A and protein, which helps your body preserve calcium and strengthen your bones.

Recommended daily intake

2-3 cups, about 16-24 fl oz (about 470-710ml)
